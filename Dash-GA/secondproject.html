Salmon quinoa salad

  1. 1. (8-ounce) salmon fillet (such as wild Alaskan)
  2. 2. 1 teaspoon salt
  3. 3. 1⁄2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  4. 4. 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  5. 5. 11⁄2 cooked quinoa (about 1/2 cup uncooked)
  6. 6. 1⁄2 cup chopped carrots
  7. 7. 1⁄2 chopped fresh basil leaves
  8. 8. 1⁄4 cup cooked corn
  9. 9. 2 tablespoons avocado ranch sauce

Direction

1. Sprinkle salmon evenly with 1/4 teaspoon salt and 1/8 teaspoon pepper. Heat a medium skillet over high heat. Add 1 tablespoon oil to pan. Reduce heat to medium-high; cook 4 minutes. Turn fillet; cook 2 minutes or until desired degree of doneness. Place fillet on a cutting board.Flake fillet with a fork into small chunks.

2. Combine flaked salmon, remaining 3/4 teaspoon salt, remaining 3/8 teaspoon pepper, quinoa, and remaining ingredients in a large bowl; toss gently to combine.
